Johannes ziegler

Founder and president

Carpentry and construction was a natural career for Johannes. As a boy he began making fine jewelry, mixed medium sculpture, furniture, and playing with all kinds of hand and power tools. He now gets to use tools every day and indulge in grown-up toys. From an early age he helped his Dad, an electrical engineer, run wires for fire, security, access control, and CCT. He remembers that crawling around in buildings with his Dad was an introduction to building construction.

In high school he worked for a new construction builder on a house in the Northeast kingdom. Through college he worked as a sculptural metal worker and installer, and home remodeler for an architectural firm, and also for a furniture building company. After college, his liberal arts degree was general and artsy, so he leaned into his experience, skill and love of tools and began home repair work, soon moving on to remodeling. He later trained to be a real estate home inspector which further expanded his understanding of building construction.
