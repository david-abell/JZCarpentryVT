JZ Carpentry

JZ Carpentry is a Burlington, VT based company that specializes in residential remodeling of kitchens, bathrooms, and new additions. Johannes Ziegler founded the company in 2009 to focus on natural, healthy, energy efficient, and locally sourced renovations. His crew, team of trade specialists, and his network of materials suppliers bring a personal touch and attention to detail to your upcoming projects.

Johannes lives with his wife and three cats. When he is not working, he enjoys paragliding, back country skiing, and brewing his own hard cider.
