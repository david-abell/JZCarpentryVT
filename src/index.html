JZ Carpentry

JZ Carpentry is a Burlington, VT based remodeling company that specializes in general contracting, residential renovations, new construction, additions, and tiny houses. Johannes Ziegler founded the company in 2009 and runs the business from his home office. He general contracts building projects and works on site with his crew and support teams of local subcontractors who excel in their specialty trades.
